About VitaHeart

VitaHeart uses Naïve Bayesian and SVM classifiers trained on the UCI Heart Disease dataset to predict the likelihood of cardiovascular disease from 13 clinical attributes: age, sex, chest pain type, resting blood pressure, serum cholesterol, fasting blood sugar, resting ECG results, maximum heart rate, exercise-induced angina, ST depression (oldpeak), slope of ST segment, number of major vessels, and thalassemia status.

The dataset (303 records) is split 80/20 for training and testing. After authentication, patients enter their clinical parameters and receive an immediate binary prediction — presence or absence of heart disease — along with a model accuracy comparison chart.

For educational and research purposes only. Does not constitute medical advice.
